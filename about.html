About Me

Creative Technologist & Storyteller

Hi! I’m Lilly Majors—a creative technologist, storyteller, and interactive media designer. I graduated with a BA in Strategic Communications from Kansas State University, where I gained a strong foundation in writing, research, audience engagement, and digital media strategy. I’m now pursuing an MSc in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College Dublin, where I’m exploring XR/AR, web development, 3D modeling, animation, and multimedia production.

My path has taken me from strategic communications and content development to teaching English abroad and founding an extended reality club—and now into interactive design. Across every step, I’ve been driven by curiosity, creativity, and a desire to bring ideas to life. I’m especially drawn to work that blends storytelling with technology, whether through designing digital experiences, creating immersive content, or experimenting with new tools and platforms.

Originally from Kansas—a place rich with jazz, sunflowers, and barbecue—I’m still discovering where this journey will take me. I don’t have one fixed answer to what I’ll “be” when I grow up, but I know I’m meant to create, explore, and keep learning along the way.
